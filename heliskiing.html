Heli Skiing

Heli Skiing

We all love the feeling of gliding down an entire run without a care in the world, without encountering any other skiers or boarders. But how often does it happen? Heli-skiing is the only way you can guarantee to have slope after slope to yourselves. The peace and quiet you experience is out of this world – you’d quite easily make the assumption you’re the only people on the planet!
You’ll be bouncing though deep powder like you wouldn’t believe. A snorkel won’t appear on the kit list, but you might need one! You’re dropped off at the top of those beautiful mountains and it’s guaranteed your tracks will be the only ones on the side of the mountain!

Places To Explore

  • Himachal Pradesh
  • Kashmir
  • Uttaranchal

Day 01
Arrive at the nearest airport/station. Our cabs will take you to our 5 star hotel. You can freshen up and relax for the day (Food will be served at the hotel)

Day 02
Today is a training day. Virtual as well as physical training will be given at the Heli-skiing station. All the safety precautions will be taught by experts

Day 03
The Big Day arrives! Get ready for the most exciting adventure of your life. Today you go Heli-Skiing!

Day 04
Visit the town and enjoy the place. Visit the market. Overnight relaxation at the hotel

Day 05
A great trip ends with lots of memories to take back home!
