Sky Diving

Sky Diving

It’s such a surreal feeling. From the pilot letting you know that you’re about to reach the perfect jumping spot, to realizing that it’s time for you and your tandem instructor to attach your harnesses and get in position. From the moment the door opens and you notice how high you are, to the initial plunge and everything else that follows- the entire experience is a memory that you’ll carry with you forever. Life is for living, right? At least with skydiving there’s a cooler story.
