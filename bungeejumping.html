Bungee Jumping

The first and foremost motive to bungee jump: Get over your fear of heights! Forgetting everything else, you dive into the open space towards the earth, that’s when you know your fear of heights is gone for life… How we build up each and everything to large sizes and how tiny everything is! This you realize only when you see the Earth from 400 feet. It’s freaking and amazing at the same time. Do try it at least once!
